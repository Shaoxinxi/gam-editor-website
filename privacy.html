Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Summary: GAM Vehicle Editor is a local desktop application. It does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or game files to any server. Your privacy is protected by design.

1. Information We Collect

GAM Vehicle Editor ("the Software") operates primarily as a local application. We collect minimal information:

  • Purchase information: When you purchase a license, payment processing is handled by Paddle. We receive your email address for license delivery and support purposes.
  • Crash reports (optional): If you choose to submit a bug report through the in-app tool, the report may include system information and application logs. This is entirely optional.
  • Anonymous usage statistics: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data (e.g., which game modules are used) to improve the Software. No personally identifiable information is included.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access, read, or transmit your game save files or personal game data.
  • We do not collect your name, address, or financial information (handled entirely by Paddle).
  • We do not track your browsing history or behavior outside the Software.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Deliver and activate your software license.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve the Software based on aggregated, anonymous feedback.
  • Send important product updates and security notices to your registered email.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle – Payment processing and license management. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare – Website hosting and content delivery. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your email address and license information are stored securely. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and license information for as long as your license is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for support purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@gam-editor.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Software is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
