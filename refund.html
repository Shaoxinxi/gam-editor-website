Refund Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Our commitment: We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you experience a technical issue we cannot resolve, we will work with you to find a solution or provide a refund.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new license purchases. If you are not satisfied with the Software for any reason within 14 days of your purchase, you may request a full refund.

2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

  • The Software does not function as described on the website.
  • You experience a technical issue that our support team is unable to resolve within a reasonable timeframe.
  • The Software is incompatible with your system and we cannot provide a fix.
  • You accidentally purchased a duplicate license.
  • You request a refund within 14 days of purchase and have not extensively used the Software.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Refund requests made more than 14 days after purchase.
  • Requests based on incompatibility with a game version that was released after the Software's stated system requirements.
  • Requests where the license key has been shared or misused.
  • Change of mind after extensive use (more than 10 save operations).

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at support@gam-editor.com with:

  • Your order number or purchase email address.
  • A brief description of the issue or reason for the refund request.

We will process your request within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

5. Subscription Cancellation

If you have purchased a subscription plan, you may cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not provide partial refunds for unused subscription time.

6. Disputes

If you are unsatisfied with our response to a refund request, you may escalate the dispute through Paddle's buyer protection program. We are committed to resolving all disputes fairly.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at: support@gam-editor.com
